Formulas & method

Non‑constrained (IBC Eq. 18‑1, 18‑2):

d = 0.5·A·[1 + √(1 + 4.36·h / A)]

A = 2.34·P / (S₁·b)

where S₁ is the allowable lateral bearing at ⅓ of the embedment depth, so the solution is found by iteration.

Constrained (IBC Eq. 18‑3):

d = √(4.25·P·h / (S₃·b))

where S₃ is the allowable lateral bearing at the full embedment depth.

Inputs are converted to US‑customary units, the code equations are applied, and the result is converted back to metres. Conversions: 1 m = 3.28084 ft, 1 kN = 224.809 lb, 1 kN/m³ = 6.366 psf/ft.

For preliminary design only. Verify against IBC 2021 §1806/§1807 and project geotechnical data. Embedment for round/square posts; the deflection basis is 12 mm (0.5 in) at grade.
